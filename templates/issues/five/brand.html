You're so Fucking Valid

I don't want to be valid, I want
to be the asteroid
that ends the dinosaurs,
watching them stare at the sky
at the most beautiful thing
coming to destroy them, I want to be 
languageless feeling, invisible 
light, a sculpture of time,
don't call me valid, call me through your skin,
we can shed together, show me your air
and I'll breathe with you.
