Submission Guidelines

What kind of work is Trestle Ties looking for?

We examine all work received. We consider original poems written in the English language as well as translations of poetry into English. We regret that we cannot give individual criticism.

Does Trestle Ties accept previously published work?

No. We cannot consider anything that has been previously published or accepted for publication, anywhere, in any form.

How many poems can I submit?

3 poems maxiumum, not exceeding five pages in total.

In what format should I submit?

All poems must be in a single pdf. or word document.
Poems must be separated by titles or page breaks.
Please include all writer contact info on all pages of the submitted file in the header with the writer's name, email address, and page number.

When is the next submission period?

Submissions will be open from November 15th through December 31st, 2018.

How do I submit?

Email your document to trestleties@gmail.com.
We do not use submittable or other submission systems so that you incur no costs to submit.

Other details

We will try to let you know about acceptance as soon as possible. Please give us at least a month after the submission closing datebefore inquiring about your submission.

Thank you for trusting us with your work. We hope you enjoy Trestle Ties.
